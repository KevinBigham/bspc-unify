# Closed Beta Plan

**Cohort:** 3–5 staff families using synthetic/staging first, then approved production accounts.
**Duration:** 14 consecutive days after all entry gates are evidenced.
**Entry:** onboarding proof/checklist, four installed builds, full-practice proof, live support intake, and rehearsed incident runbook.

## Script

- Day 0: consented onboarding, recovery, invite redemption, notification opt-in, support path.
- Days 1–3: schedule/announcement use and one attendance/practice workflow.
- Days 4–7: offline interruption, push tap-through, progress/meet context, feedback submission.
- Days 8–13: normal use; no coached novelty unless investigating a reported issue.
- Day 14: structured exit interview and account/data disposition confirmation.

Collect only: task completion, crash/session counts, severity, device/OS, support response time, and free-text feedback stripped of swimmer identifiers. Every P0/SEV-0 stops the clock; restart the 14-day exit window after the fix is deployed and verified.

## Exit criteria

- Zero open P0/SEV-0 or P1/SEV-1 defects.
- Crash-free sessions greater than 99.5% for the cohort.
- Recovery, invite, attendance, offline replay, push routing, and support closure each evidenced at least once.
- No cross-family access, consent bypass, duplicate import/onboarding write, or capability-token logging.
- Kevin signs the dated beta report; duration may not be waived by an agent.
